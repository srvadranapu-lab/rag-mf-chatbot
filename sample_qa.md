Q5: What is a riskometer in mutual funds?  
A: A riskometer is a standardized tool defined by SEBI to indicate the risk level of a mutual fund scheme, ranging from low to very high. Source: https://www.sebi.gov.in  
Last updated from sources: https://www.sebi.gov.in

Q8: What is NAV in mutual funds?  
A: NAV (Net Asset Value) represents the per-unit value of a mutual fund and is calculated based on the total value of assets minus liabilities. Source: https://www.amfiindia.com
